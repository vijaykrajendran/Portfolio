## Why Learn Nginx?

In my years as a DevOps engineer, I've seen Nginx power everything from tiny startups to massive enterprises. Here's why it matters:

- **High Performance**: Nginx handles millions of concurrent connections on modest hardware
- **Reverse Proxy Magic**: Route traffic to multiple backends, balance loads, and handle failovers
- **SSL/TLS Termination**: Secure all your services with HTTPS in one place
- **Production Critical**: Used by Netflix, Airbnb, Dropbox, and practically every major tech company
